Restaurants

Cristiano Ronaldo, Rafael Nadal, Pau Gasol, and singer Enrique Iglesias have a stake in Grupo Mabel Capital, which is the company behind the elite restaurant Zela. The Zela restaurant has been built in two European cities, Ibiza (Spain) and London (England).
